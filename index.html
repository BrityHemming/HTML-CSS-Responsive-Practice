Our Story

This is our story. We like plants. We water plants. Plants sometimes need food, like humans do, so we put them out in the sunlight. Plants like to eat sunlight. Some plants need more sunlight than water. Succulents are a type of plant.

Succulents

Succulents are a plant that require little watering. They live in fairly dry areas and can store water in their thick leaves. Succulents are great pets!
